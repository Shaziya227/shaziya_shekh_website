About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Psychology at Dr. Ghanshyam Singh Mahavidyalaya, Varanasi, where I have been teaching since August 2024. I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Psychology from Mahatma Gandhi Kashi Vidyapith, Varanasi.

My research focus lies at the intersection of adolescent psychology, disability studies, and mental health. I am particularly interested in how parenting styles, emotional intelligence, and social factors influence psychological resilience and wellbeing in young people with and without physical disabilities.
